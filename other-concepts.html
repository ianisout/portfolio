Graphics, Copy, and Marketing

Unbox English was a school founded in partnership with an amazing English teacher and friend. An online school with different approaches to learning. I looked after some marketing by creating posts and promotions. All graphics from these posts were designed by me, as well as a lot of its written content.

The school is gone, but you can still check out the artwork and copy on the instagram page.

IanLima.com was a website I had as an English teacher. I created content to help Brazilians in learning the language. I wrote informative pieces, tricks, and translated useful articles of various themes for Portuguese speakers — wishing to provide my compatriots with good, English-only content I had found online but was not available in Portuguese: news and articles about career, language learning, etc.

You can also check out one of the ebooks I wrote for this project — P.S.: It is in Portuguese.
